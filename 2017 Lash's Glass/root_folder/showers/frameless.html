Frameless shower doors, a step above

Frameless shower doors (or heavy glass showers) are a great way to add modern & contemporary style to your bathroom. With simplistic lines and minimal metal hardware frameless showers are an amazing addition to any bathroom remodel.

    Glass Features

  • 3/8" or 1/2" thick tempered glass
  • Minimal visible metal
  • Available with low iron glass
  • Availabe with a water protective coating
  • Clear or pattern glass
    •

    Hardware Features

  • Clamp or u-channel installation
  • Decorative handles & knobs
  • Multiple clamp & hinge styles
  • Available in multiple finishes (chrome, brushed nickel, bronze, & more)
    •
