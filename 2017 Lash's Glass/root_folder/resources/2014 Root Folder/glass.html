Glass & Mirrors

Mirror
At Lash's Glass, we can cut mirrors from 1" circles to wall length sheets for fitness centers. We love making your design a reality with equipment to polish edges and drill holes from 1/8" to 4", and glass routers to cut patterns.
We can special order your size and design -- you name it, we'll make it!

Glass
We offer glass in many thicknesses, from 1/16" thick to 1" thick. Whether it's clear, frosted, plexi-glass (acrylic), lexan (polycarbonate), etched, or textured, we have the glass you are looking for.

We can cut glass for picture frames and windows, tabletops and shelves with beautifully polished & beveled edges. We also offer glass for cabinet doors, and have a large selection of glass patterns to fit your style.
